Chicken Alfredo

Instructions

Hello and welcome to the Chicken Alfredo Recipe! Chicken Alfredo is personally one of my favorite dishes to eat. So lets get started.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 1/2 c. whole milk
